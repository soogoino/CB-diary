Chastity Diary is a private, offline-first Android app for tracking your chastity lifestyle. All data stays on your device — no internet connection is required or used.

## Features

**Daily Logging**
- Record whether you are wearing your device today
- Track mood, desire level, comfort, and mental focus with intuitive sliders
- Log sleep quality, bedtime and wake time
- Record morning and night erection data
- Optional daily photo check-in (stored locally, blurred by default in history view)
- Rotating pool of 33 optional questions to keep your journaling fresh
- Notes field for any additional thoughts

**Streaks & Statistics**
- Automatic streak tracking (longest streak, current streak)
- Dashboard with charts: mood trends, desire levels, exercise frequency, and more
- Heatmap view to visualise patterns over time

**Privacy & Security**
- PIN protection with configurable length (4–6 digits)
- Biometric unlock (fingerprint / face recognition)
- App lock: authentication required on every launch
- Photos blurred by default in history — tap to reveal
- Zero analytics, zero crash reporting, zero network access

**Data Management**
- Export all entries to a CSV file (via Android file picker)
- Import from CSV to restore or migrate data
- Fully offline — data never leaves your device unless you export it yourself

**Accessibility & Customisation**
- Full English + Traditional Chinese (繁體中文) localisation
- Dark / Light / System theme
- Morning and evening configurable reminders (local notifications only)

## Permissions Used

| Permission | Reason |
|---|---|
| CAMERA | Optional photo check-in |
| POST_NOTIFICATIONS | Daily and morning reminders |
| USE_BIOMETRIC | Biometric unlock |
| VIBRATE | Notification haptic feedback |

No INTERNET permission is requested.
